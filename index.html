About

Van life is growing fast, but DIY conversions can be tough and often end up with wasted space or impractical designs. Our project makes van conversions easier by creating simple, adaptable designs that anyone can use. Whether you’re cutting parts at a local maker space or ordering them pre-made, we’re here to help turn your van into the adventure-ready home of your dreams.
