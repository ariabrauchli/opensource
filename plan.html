Plan Your Van

Begin here in our "Plan Your Van" tab where you use our free web based Onshape CAD tool to customize your Rock N Roll Bed. Once your design is ready you have two options. The DIY option lets you download free CAD cut files so you can mill and assemble your bed yourself. We also provide a comprehensive Tips and Tricks page with a detailed assembly guide. Alternatively, choose BIY, which stands for Build It for You, where we handle the milling and deliver your fully assembled bed.
